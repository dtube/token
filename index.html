A new key player:

the curator

On DTube, content curators use their skills to reveal the best videos of the web by posting a link of a third-party video website and by voting and tagging the most relevant video posts on DTube.
By playing this curation game, curators try to grow their influence power by earning DTC tokens through their posts, votes and tags.
This mechanism rewards influencers in a new way that hasn’t existed before.

The DTube Coin: a utility token for video

For players: hold your DTC to post, vote, comment, tag videos and earn rewards
For fans (live): Support your favorite creators and curators with the “donate DTC” feature
For advertisers (live): Burn DTC to promote videos on the platform
For viewers (2020): Spend your DTC to access premium content

DTube Coin distribution: how it works

The DTube Chain distributes Voting Power (VP) to every DTuber at a rate of +1 per DTC per hour.
DTubers spend their VP each time they post, vote, comment or tag a content.
In real time, the blockchain algorithm generates and distributes new DTC to users to reward them according to content popularity and freshness.
